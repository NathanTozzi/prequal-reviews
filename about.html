About This Site

Our commitment to transparency, honest reviews, and helping GCs find the right prequalification software

Who We Are

PrequalReviews is owned and operated by Highwire, a subcontractor prequalification and risk management platform for general contractors.

We created this site because we believe the construction industry deserves better resources for evaluating software—especially in the niche category of subcontractor prequalification. While sites like G2 and Capterra cover thousands of software categories, they often lack the construction-specific context that GCs, risk managers, and project teams need when making purchasing decisions.

Why We Built This

We know this space intimately because we're in it. Our goal is to educate the market, provide genuine comparisons, and help you find the best platform for your needs—even if that platform isn't Highwire.

Transparency & Disclosure

We believe transparency builds trust. Here's what you need to know:

  • Highwire owns this site. This is not an "independent" third-party review site. Highwire is featured prominently on the homepage because we own it.
  • We feature our competitors. You'll find other platforms listed here, including ISNetworld, Avetta, and others. We describe them fairly and accurately.
  • We're FTC-compliant. All ownership and potential conflicts of interest are clearly disclosed throughout the site. We do not post fake reviews or misleading "rankings."
  • This site generates leads for Highwire. When you fill out forms or request demos, Highwire's team may contact you. That's the business model—we're upfront about it.

FTC Compliance Notice

In accordance with Federal Trade Commission guidelines on endorsements and reviews, we disclose that this site is owned by Highwire and may generate leads and revenue for Highwire. All reviews published on this site are from verified sources and are not fabricated or incentivized.

How Reviews Work

Who Can Submit Reviews

We accept reviews from verified users of prequalification platforms, including:

  • General contractors and construction firm owners
  • Risk managers and safety directors
  • Project managers and operations teams
  • CFOs, finance teams, and procurement professionals

Our Review Verification Process

All reviews go through a verification process before being published:

  1. Submission: Users submit reviews via our review form, including their name, role, company, and platform experience.
  2. Verification: We validate that the reviewer is a real person at a real company. We may contact reviewers via email or LinkedIn to confirm their identity and experience.
  3. Moderation: Reviews are checked for compliance with our guidelines (no profanity, no spam, must be based on actual usage).
  4. Publication: Once verified, reviews are published with the reviewer's name, role, and company (unless they request anonymity for legitimate reasons).

Rating Criteria

Platforms are rated on five key dimensions:

  • Ease of Use: How intuitive the platform is for both GC admins and subcontractors
  • Implementation & Support: Onboarding speed and ongoing support quality
  • Insurance & Compliance Features: Automation, accuracy, and reporting capabilities
  • Integrations: Connectivity with existing ERP and project management tools
  • Value for Money: Pricing transparency and ROI justification

Ratings are calculated as the average of verified reviews in each category. Highwire's "Featured" status is based on ownership, not on inflated ratings.

Editorial Independence

While Highwire owns this site, we maintain editorial standards to ensure accuracy and fairness:

  • Competitor descriptions are factual. We describe other platforms based on publicly available information and verified user feedback.
  • We don't fabricate negative reviews of competitors. All negative reviews (of any platform, including Highwire) must come from verified users.
  • We don't suppress negative reviews of Highwire. If a verified user has a legitimate complaint about Highwire, we publish it.
  • We prioritize helpfulness over sales. Our goal is to educate buyers so they make informed decisions, which builds long-term trust and credibility.

Dispute Process

If you believe a review is inaccurate, fraudulent, or violates our guidelines:

  1. Email us at reviews@highwire.com with the review URL and a detailed explanation of your concern.
  2. We'll investigate within 5 business days and respond with our findings.
  3. If the review is found to be fraudulent or in violation of our policies, we'll remove it.
  4. If the review is legitimate but you disagree with it, you can submit a public response that will be published alongside the review.
